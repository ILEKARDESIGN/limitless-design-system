Best for → Cleaner bar alternatives · Range comparisons · Before/after
Replaces thick bars with thin stems and terminal dots for a lighter visual weight. The dumbbell variant shows two endpoints connected by a line for range or change comparisons. Covers lollipop, dumbbell, connected dot plot, and dot bar hybrid.

Best for → Cumulative contribution · P&L breakdown · Sequential changes
Shows how sequential positive and negative values contribute to a total. Cyan for totals, yellow for increases, pink for decreases. Covers waterfall, bridge chart, and Pareto chart. Dashed connectors link floating segments.

Mirror-image horizontal bars extending from a central axis, comparing two populations or variables. Uses pink for one side and cyan for the other, creating a visually striking diverging pattern. Covers tornado chart, population pyramid, and diverging bar chart.

Scatter / Bubble / Regression / Hexbin
Best for → Correlation · Clustering · Outlier detection · Multi-variable
Plots individual data points on two axes to reveal relationships. Covers scatter plot, bubble chart (size = third variable), connected scatter, regression with trendline, hexbin, and contour plot. Uses neon dots on dark backgrounds with dashed yellow trendlines. Orange marks outliers. Bubble size encodes a third variable.

Best for → Multi-dimensional comparison · Skill profiles · Assessment scoring
Plots multiple variables on radial axes emanating from a centre point. Covers radar chart, spider chart, star plot, petal chart, and radial parallel coordinates. Pink filled polygon with cyan dashed overlay for comparison. Grid rings at diminishing opacity.
