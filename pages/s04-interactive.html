The 12 Jobs of
Visual Data in UI
Every visual data presentation falls into one or more of these fundamental jobs.
1
Show a Single Metric
KPI card, stat, big number, gauge, progress ring
2
Compare Categories
Bar, column, lollipop, dumbbell, slope, radar
3
Show Change Over Time
Line, area, sparkline, step, bump, timeline
4
Show Part-to-Whole
Pie, donut, treemap, waffle, sunburst, stacked bar
5
Show Distribution
Histogram, density, box plot, violin, beeswarm
6
Show Relationships
Scatter, bubble, correlation matrix, parallel coords
7
Show Flow
Sankey, alluvial, chord, funnel, user journey
8
Show Hierarchy
Tree, dendrogram, org chart, sunburst, treemap
9
Show Location
Choropleth, bubble map, heatmap, pin map, flow map
10
Show Status / Progress
Progress bar, gauge, status dot, traffic light
11
Show Operations
Gantt, Kanban, roster, calendar, burndown
12
Show Detail Records
Table, pivot table, audit log, ledger, scorecard

THE LIMITLESS DECISION FRAMEWORK
Start with the job your data needs to do — not the chart name. Ask: "What job am I hiring this visualization to do?" Then select the specific chart type from that job's family.
