Budget Envelope / Compartmentalized Budget View
Best for → NDIS plan budget management · Three-state financial tracking
The single most important widget in an NDIS CRM. Shows committed (booked but unspent), spent (claimed), and available (remaining) simultaneously across all NDIS budget categories. Three-state stacked bars: pink/cyan/yellow = spent, orange = committed, transparent = available. Each row shows total allocation, absolute dollar amounts, and percentage breakdown. Fundamentally different from a simple progress bar because it encodes three financial states, not two. Also covers plan period countdown banner, utilization speedometer, projected exhaustion date indicator, and over/under utilization alert card.
Critical CRM Design Notes
Three-state bar: spent (solid neon) + committed (orange at 60–70%) + available (track) · 14px bar height for text embedding · JetBrains Mono 6px inline labels · Category-specific colours: pink = Core, cyan = CB, yellow = Capital · Total $ right-aligned in Bebas Neue · Pacing indicator compares budget % vs plan elapsed %

Spend Pacing / Budget Forecast Projection
Best for → Budget trajectory · Early warning · Spend rate analysis
Time-series comparing actual spend curve against ideal linear pacing with projected trajectory. Pink solid for actuals, dashed pink for projection, dashed white for ideal pace, cyan today marker. Pink-tinted zone flags projected overrun. Covers budget forecast projection, spend-vs-time pacing chart, plan period elapsed vs budget consumed dual gauge, and projected exhaustion date indicator.

Service Period Overlap Timeline
Best for → Concurrent service tracking · Agreement periods · Gap detection
Horizontal multi-row timeline showing concurrent service agreements stacked per participant with plan period overlay. Different from Gantt because it shows overlap/conflict rather than dependency. Yellow dashed outline = plan period, coloured bars = active service agreements, yellow markers = SA expiry dates. Reveals service gaps (empty zones) and overlaps (where bars extend beyond plan). Also covers service agreement status board, appointment conflict detector, and service gap analysis chart.

Compliance RAG Matrix / Audit Readiness Scorecard
Best for → Caseload compliance overview · Audit preparation · Gap identification
Participant × compliance item grid where each cell is a coloured square: cyan = current, orange = due soon, pink = overdue/missing. Different from a generic risk matrix because axes are entity-based (participants × compliance items), not variable-based. At a glance across your caseload, you see exactly what's overdue. Also covers consent form tracker, practice standards compliance checklist, NDIS Commission matter tracker, conflict of interest register, safeguarding alert panel, and audit readiness scorecard. The incident/risk trend sparkline grid variant embeds mini sparklines into each cell showing trend direction.

Goal Progress Staircase / Milestone Tracker
Best for → NDIS goal achievement · Discrete milestone tracking · Non-linear progress
Ascending steps representing discrete milestones toward a goal. Filled steps = achieved, outlined dashed = next target, dimmed = future. Yellow marker shows current position. Different from a progress bar because goals aren't linear percentages — they're qualitative milestones with evidence. Also covers goal milestone timeline, capacity building progress matrix, and goal-to-support linkage map.

Communication Thread Timeline / Case Notes Log
Best for → Participant interaction history · Audit trail · Billable time tracking
Unified chronological stream of all communications with type-coded icons, timestamps, billable hours, and expandable content. Pink = email, cyan = phone, yellow = case note, purple = meeting. The vertical timeline line with dot markers creates a visual thread. Different from an activity feed because it's bidirectional (sent/received), includes billable time, and is auditable. Also covers billable time tracker, multi-channel unified inbox, case note template selector, interaction duration bar chart, follow-up reminder stack, SC activity summary dashboard, participant engagement score, participant interaction heatmap, communication type breakdown, and handover briefing card.
CRM-Specific Design Notes
Vertical 2px timeline border-left · 12px coloured dot markers with embedded SVG type icons · JetBrains Mono 6px type/date/billable labels · Progressive opacity for older entries · Billable hours tagged inline · Expandable content on click · Filterable by type
